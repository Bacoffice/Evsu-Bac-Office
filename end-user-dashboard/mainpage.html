The Bids and Awards Committee (BAC) Office is a vital unit within a school or university that is responsible for overseeing the procurement process for goods, services, and construction projects. This office plays a critical role in ensuring that the procurement process is transparent, efficient, and in compliance with government regulations and guidelines. The BAC Office is responsible for conducting the bidding process, selecting the most qualified suppliers or contractors, and ensuring that the school or university obtains the best value for its money. As such, the BAC Office is an essential component of any school or university that is committed to fair and accountable procurement practices.
